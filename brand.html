When did Toco first appear in the United States?

This daily staple was filling, delicious, and affordable. The Taco was first introduced to the United States in 1905. Mexican migrants were coming in to work on railroads and other jobs and started to bring their delicious food with them. 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
